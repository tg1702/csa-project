Confused About Counters?

We're here to help

Our Goal

Our aim at 3-2-1 Counters is to develop a simple, user-friendly website to teach Computer Systems Architecture students about synchronous counters using J-K, T and D flip-flops

Where do I start?

Our Learn section is where you should head! We have a collection of carefully curated notes covering all the topics you need to know to build a website. If you're not confident about your binary conversion or your Boolean algebra skills, you can head to the prerequisite section before you move on.

After you have the prerequisites covered, we recommend you cover the rest of the learn section, which will be an in-depth look at building counters using flip-flops. Every section will have a set of objectives to cover and a couple of multiple choice questions based on the section material just to make sure you've thoroughly read and understood the content.

Test by Gaming!

Our unique Test section uses a game to test your knowledge on building synchronous counters!
